Fill Up: Chicken Breast

Fill up in a variety of ways

An 8 oz. chicken breast, mashed potatoes, biscuit, medium drink and a chocolate chip cookie.

  • Try it in a variety of ways:
  • Meal #1 2 pc. Drum & Thigh Combo: 1 Drum, 1 Thigh, Individual Mashed Potatoes & Gravy, Biscuit, Medium Drink
  • Meal #9 3, 4, or 5 pc. Tenders: 3, 4, or 5 Tenders, 1 Side, Biscuit, Medium Drink
  • Meal #15Pot Pie Fill Up: Pot Pie, Cookie, Medium Drink
  • Meal #16Famous Bowl Fill Up: Famous Bowl, Cookie, Medium Drink
